About Us

Our Story

SawaSawa Academy is a multi-disciplinary school commited to equiping it's students with the necessary skills required for the job market. It was started in 2011 with an aim of providing the students with a good and confortable learning environment also to give them the necessary assistance to achieve their educational goals.

Mission

SawaSawa Academy provides a well-rounded education that fulfill the needs of the whole student, developing the attitude, knowledge, understanding and skills for students to become mindful and ethical global citizens.

Vision

As a progressive technology school, we journey through learning together, creating an environment where students love to seek knowledge, follow their passion, and are called to action.

Core Values

Respect
We show respect by speaking and acting with courtesy. We treat others with dignity and honor the rules of our family, school, and nation. Respect yourself, and others will respect you.

Honesty
We are honest when we are truthful and sincere. It is important because it builds trust. When people are honest, they can be relied on to not lie, cheat or steal. Being honest means that you accept yourself as you are. When you are open and trustworthy, others can believe in you.
Self-Discipline
We show self-discipline when we exercise self-control. It is being who you really are and doing what you really want to do, rather than being tossed around by your feelings like a leaf in the wind. You act, instead of reacting. You get things done in an orderly and efficient way. You are self-directed. With self-discipline, you take charge of yourself.
